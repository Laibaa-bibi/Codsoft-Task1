Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Date of Birth

Muhammad Ali Jinnah was born on 25 December 1876

Education

He studied at the Sindh-Madrasa-tul-Islam and the
Christian Missionary Society High School
in Karachi. Later, he completed his matriculation
from Bombay University at the high school.

Honors

American historian Stanley Wolpert,
giving a speech in honour of Jinnah in 1998
deemed him Pakistan's greatest leader.
